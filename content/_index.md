My work sits at the boundary of **scaling embodied intelligence** and **making that scaling legible** to a non-technical audience: from pretraining and fine-tuning VLAs through structured evaluations on real hardware to assess whether a robotics stack is actually deployment-ready.

I am a Senior AI Research Engineer at **Agility Robotics**, building practical paths from data to reliable policies on humanoid platforms. Previously, I spent years developing and training AI models for all kinds of autonomous systems including drones, AVs, robotics-arms, mobile manipulators, and humanoids; translating research into rollouts, metrics, and the glue that keeps sensors, labels, and evaluation honest.

### Core expertise

**Robot learning**  
Imitation learning at scale; VLA pretraining and fine-tuning, Reinforcement Learning on real hardware, World Models

**Systems & deployment**  
Sim-to-real transfer, automated evaluation loops, and data-quality pipelines that connect messy tele-op and fleet logs to models you can trust enough to run unattended.

**Independent technical advisory**  
Technical due diligence, maturity workshops, and strategic advisory for VCs/PEs and expert networks (independent of any employer). See the [Advisory](/advisory/) page for scope, disclaimer, and representative client channels.
