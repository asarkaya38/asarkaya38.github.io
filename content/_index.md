My work sits at the boundary of **scaling embodied intelligence** and **making that scaling legible** to investors and operators: from imitation learning and VLA fine-tuning on real hardware to structured assessments of when a robotics stack is actually deployment-ready.

I am a Senior AI Research Engineer in embodied AI at **Agility Robotics** (San Jose), building practical paths from data to reliable policies on humanoid platforms. Previously, I spent several years shipping manipulation and learning stacks across arms and mobile manipulators in the field—translating research into rollouts, metrics, and the unglamorous glue that keeps sensors, labels, and evaluation honest.

### Core expertise

**Robot learning**  
Imitation learning at scale, VLA fine-tuning and robust training regimes, and policy families (e.g. diffusion-style and transformer-based controllers) grounded in real robot data—not slide decks.

**Systems & deployment**  
Sim-to-real transfer, automated evaluation loops, and data-quality pipelines that connect messy tele-op and fleet logs to models you can trust enough to run unattended.

**Independent technical advisory**  
Technical due diligence, maturity workshops, and strategic advisory for funds and expert networks—always **independent** of any employer. See the [Advisory](/advisory/) page for scope, disclaimer, and representative client channels.
